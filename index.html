How do I get started with ProQueue?

Start with a workflow audit and live sandbox session. We map your call types, dispatch rules, and booking goals, then launch in phased rollout.

What if a caller asks for a live teammate?

ProQueue hands off to your team instantly when confidence drops or a customer asks for a person. Every handoff includes full context so no one restarts the call.

How many calls can ProQueue handle at once?

ProQueue is built for concurrency. Whether you receive 10 calls per hour or 10,000, routing and response quality remain consistent during peaks.

How does ProQueue help us capture every new customer?

It combines instant response, lead qualification, and structured follow-up to reduce missed opportunities across nights, weekends, and overflow windows.

Can ProQueue run outbound calls and send texts?

Yes. You can launch automated follow-up sequences for unsold estimates, unbooked leads, and membership renewals from one orchestration layer.

Does ProQueue integrate with our existing software?

Yes. ProQueue supports direct and API-based integrations with ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, HubSpot, Zapier, and internal tools.

Will ProQueue work with our current dispatch and management tools?

In most cases, yes. If your stack has API access or webhook support, we can sync events, notes, and booking outcomes without forcing a platform migration.
